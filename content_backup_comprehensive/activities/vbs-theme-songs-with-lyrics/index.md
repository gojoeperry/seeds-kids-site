## Worship Ideas
1. "God's Love Is Like a Lighthouse" - Have children make wave motions with their arms during the verses and form a lighthouse shape by raising their arms together during the chorus.

2. "Jesus' Power Pulls Us Through" - Create a train formation where kids hold onto each other's shoulders and move around the room, stopping to do specific motions at key lyrics.

3. "Kingdom Rock" - Give children colorful scarves to wave during worship, encouraging them to create different patterns based on the song's rhythm and words.

### FAQs

### What age group are VBS theme songs most appropriate for?
VBS theme songs are typically designed for children ages 4-12, but they can be enjoyed by all ages. Younger children might focus more on the motions and simple choruses, while older kids can understand deeper lyrics and lead younger ones.

### How can I help my child remember VBS songs throughout the year?
Create a playlist of VBS songs to play during car rides or daily routines. You can also incorporate the songs into bedtime routines, family devotions, or make up your own motions to keep the songs fresh and engaging.

### Where can I find quality VBS theme songs with lyrics?
Many Christian publishers offer VBS music packages with lyric sheets and motion guides. Popular sources include LifeWay, Group Publishing, and Orange Kids. You can also find many VBS songs with lyrics on YouTube channels dedicated to children's ministry.
