## Worship Ideas
1. "God's Love Is Like a Lighthouse" - Have children make wave motions with their arms during verses and form a lighthouse shape during the chorus.

2. "Jesus' Team" - Create simple cheerleading moves that reinforce biblical concepts. Kids can spell out J-E-S-U-S with their bodies.

3. "Bible Heroes March" - Let children march in place while singing about different Bible characters, striking a pose for each hero mentioned.

### FAQs

Q: What age group is best suited for VBS theme songs?
A: VBS songs typically work well for children ages 4-12, though they can be adapted for younger or older groups. The key is choosing songs with age-appropriate lyrics and movements that match their developmental stage.

Q: How can I help my child remember VBS songs throughout the year?
A: Create a playlist of VBS songs to play during car rides, incorporate them into bedtime routines, and use hand motions while singing together. Consider making simple music videos with your phone to review the moves.

Q: Where can I find quality VBS theme songs and resources?
A: Many Christian publishers offer complete VBS music packages, including LifeWay, Group Publishing, and Orange. You can also find collections on streaming platforms like Spotify or YouTube Kids, or through your local Christian bookstore.
