## Worship Ideas
1. Bible Verse Bounce: Have children bounce a ball while singing each word of the verse, helping them remember the rhythm and words.
2. Scripture Sign Language: Teach simple hand motions for key words in each verse-song, making it more interactive and memorable.
3. Musical Memory Circle: Form a circle and pass a Bible while singing the scripture song, stopping the music randomly for children to recite the verse.
