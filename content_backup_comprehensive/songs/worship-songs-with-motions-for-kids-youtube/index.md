## Worship Ideas
1. "Every Move I Make" - Lead children in jumping, spinning, and raising hands while singing about following Jesus. Perfect for preschool through elementary ages.

2. "This Little Light of Mine" - Add motions like finger-pointing, walking in place, and shining actions. Great for teaching about being Jesus' light in the world.

3. "Father Abraham" - A classic action song that gets everyone moving with increasingly complex motions, teaching about faith and God's promises.

### FAQs

Q: What age group is best for worship songs with motions?
A: Most motion-based worship songs work well for children ages 2-10. Younger children enjoy simple movements, while older kids can handle more complex actions and deeper lyrics.

Q: How can I incorporate motion songs into our family devotional time?
A: Start with one song during family prayer time, teach the motions gradually, and make it a regular part of your routine. Consider creating a playlist of favorite worship songs with motions that your family can enjoy together.

Q: Where can I find quality worship songs with motions for kids on YouTube?
