## Worship Ideas
1. Scripture Song Motions: Add simple hand gestures to each key phrase in Patti Vaillant songs, helping kids connect actions with words.
2. Bible Verse Echo: Have children repeat each line after the leader, gradually building up to singing the entire verse together.
3. Memory Verse Challenge: Create friendly competitions where kids earn stickers or small rewards for learning new scripture songs and performing them for the group.
