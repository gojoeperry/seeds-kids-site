### FAQs

### What age should children start learning worship songs?
Children can begin learning worship songs as early as age 2-3. Even babies benefit from hearing praise music! Start with simple songs with repetitive phrases and gradually introduce more complex songs as they grow.

### How can I make worship time more engaging for my kids?
Make worship interactive by adding hand motions, instruments, or dance movements. Create a special worship corner in your home with instruments, ribbons, or scarves. Set regular worship times and let children take turns leading songs.
