### FAQs

### What age should children start learning worship songs?
Children can start learning worship songs as early as age 2-3. Even babies benefit from hearing simple praise songs. Start with short, repetitive songs and gradually introduce more complex ones as they grow.

### How can I make worship time more engaging for kids?
Make worship interactive by adding hand motions, using props like ribbons or instruments, and letting kids take turns leading. Keep sessions short (10-15 minutes), vary the pace between fast and slow songs, and praise their participation.
