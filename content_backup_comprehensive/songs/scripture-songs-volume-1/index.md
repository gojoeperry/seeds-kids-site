## Worship Ideas
1. "The Bible Verse Bounce" - Have children jump or bounce while singing each word of the verse, emphasizing key words with bigger movements.

2. "Echo Scripture" - Leader sings one line, children repeat it back, gradually building the complete verse through call-and-response.

3. "Scripture Sign Language" - Teach simple hand motions for key words in each verse, helping kinesthetic learners engage with the text.
