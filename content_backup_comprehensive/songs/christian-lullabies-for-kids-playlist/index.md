### FAQs

**What age should I start using Christian lullabies with my child?**
You can begin incorporating Christian lullabies from birth. Babies respond to gentle music, and establishing this routine early helps create positive sleep associations while introducing faith concepts naturally.

**How can I make bedtime worship more meaningful for my active child?**
Include simple actions or quiet movements during slower songs, let your child choose their favorite lullabies, and create a special "worship wind-down" routine with dimmed lights and soft music. Consistency and gentle engagement help active children transition to rest.

**Where can I find quality Christian lullabies for my playlist?**
Look for Christian children's artists like Michael W. Smith's lullaby albums, Hidden in My Heart Scripture Lullabies, or Sovereign Grace Kids. Many streaming platforms offer curated Christian lullaby playlists, and your local Christian bookstore likely carries bedtime worship collections.
