Christmas Bible Story Songs for Kids

Introduction

Christmas bible story songs offer a delightful way to help children understand and remember the true meaning of Christmas through music and movement. These special songs bring the nativity story to life, making it easier for young hearts to connect with Jesus’s birth in a meaningful way. When children learn christmas bible story songs, they’re not just memorizing lyrics â€“ they’re storing precious biblical truths in their hearts that can last a lifetime. The combination of familiar melodies, simple words, and biblical narratives creates an engaging learning experience that makes spiritual education both fun and effective. Whether used at home, in Sunday school, or during children’s church services, these songs help build a strong foundation of faith while celebrating the joy of Christ’s birth.

Scripture

“But the angel said to them, ‘Do not be afraid. I bring you good news that will cause great joy for all the people. Today in the town of David a Savior has been born to you; he is the Messiah, the Lord.’” - Luke 2:10-11

Worship Ideas

  1. “Away in a Manger” with hand motions:
  • “Rock arms like holding a baby during “away in a manger”
  • “Point up for “the stars in the bright sky”
  • “Fold hands in prayer for “be near me, Lord Jesus”
  1. “The First Noel” with movement:
  • “Make star shapes with hands while singing”
  • “March in place during the chorus”
  • “Create angel wings with arms during angel verses”
  1. “Silent Night” with flashlight stars:
  • “Dim lights and give children safe LED tea lights”
  • “Wave lights slowly during the chorus”
  • “Create a peaceful atmosphere for reflection”

Devotional Thought

Just as the shepherds and wise men came to worship baby Jesus, we can worship Him today through our songs and actions. Jesus came as a tiny baby to show us God’s amazing love. When we sing christmas bible story songs, we’re telling the same wonderful story that angels sang on that first Christmas night. Let’s remember that Jesus is the greatest gift we could ever receive.

Prayer: “Dear Jesus, thank you for coming as a baby to show us God’s love. Help us worship you with joy as we sing about your birth. Amen.”

FAQs

  1. What age group are Christmas Bible story songs best suited for? Christmas Bible story songs can be enjoyed by children of all ages, but they’re especially effective for ages 2-10. Toddlers and preschoolers love the actions and simple melodies, while older children can understand deeper meanings in the lyrics.

  2. How can I teach Christmas Bible story songs to wiggly children? Break the song into small sections, add hand motions or body movements, and practice one part at a time. Use visual aids like nativity scenes or pictures, and make it interactive by letting children take turns leading motions.

  3. Where can I find quality Christmas Bible story songs for kids? Look for resources from Christian children’s ministry publishers like Group Publishing or LifeWay Kids. Many churches also offer children’s worship albums, and platforms like YouTube Kids have numerous Christian channels featuring Christmas worship songs for children.
