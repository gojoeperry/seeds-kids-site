Christmas Songs for Kids with Lyrics and Action on YouTube

Introduction

Looking for engaging Christmas songs for kids with lyrics and action on YouTube? You’re in the right place! These interactive worship songs help children celebrate the true meaning of Christmas while having fun and staying active. When kids combine music, movement, and praise, they create lasting memories and develop a deeper connection with Jesus. YouTube Christmas songs for kids with lyrics and action provide an accessible way for families and children’s ministry leaders to bring the joy of worship into any setting. These songs help children express their faith through movement, making the Christmas story come alive in their hearts and minds. Plus, the visual lyrics make it easy for kids to follow along and learn the words quickly.

Worship Ideas

  1. “Joy to the World” with Star Jumps
  • “Jump up with arms raised during “Joy!”
  • “Make a circle with arms for “World”
  • “Twirl around during “Let earth receive her King”
  1. “Away in a Manger” with Gentle Motions
  • “Rock baby Jesus during verses”
  • “Make twinkling stars with fingers”
  • “Fold hands in prayer during “stay by my side”
  1. “Little Drummer Boy” with Rhythm Actions
  • “Pat legs for drum beats”
  • “March in place while singing”
  • “Use real toy drums if available”

Devotional Thought

Jesus came as a baby to show us God’s amazing love. When we sing Christmas songs, we’re celebrating His birthday and telling others about His gift of salvation. Just like the angels sang to announce Jesus’s birth, we can use our voices and actions to praise Him too. Every time we sing and move to worship songs, we’re showing Jesus how much we love Him.

Dear Jesus, thank you for coming as a baby and for giving us music to praise you. Help us share your joy with others through our songs and actions. Amen.

FAQs

Q: What age group are these action songs best suited for? A: These Christmas worship songs with actions are ideal for children ages 2-10. Younger children enjoy the movements, while older kids can understand the lyrics’ meaning and help lead others.

Q: How can I incorporate these songs into our family devotion time? A: Start with one song before bedtime or during morning routines. Practice the motions together, discuss the lyrics’ meaning, and make it a special family worship moment. You can also use them during advent calendar activities.

Q: Where can I find the best Christmas worship videos for kids? A: Look for channels like “Kids Worship Songs,” “Seeds Family Worship,” or “Go Fish Kids” on YouTube. Many churches and Christian children’s ministries also offer free playlists of Christmas songs with actions and lyrics.
