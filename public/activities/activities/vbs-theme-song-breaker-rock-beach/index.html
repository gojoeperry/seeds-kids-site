VBS Theme Song: Breaker Rock Beach

Introduction

Get ready to make waves of joy with the exciting VBS theme song “Breaker Rock Beach”! This high-energy anthem captures the spirit of summer fun while teaching kids important biblical truths. The VBS theme song Breaker Rock Beach combines catchy melodies with ocean-themed lyrics that help children remember God’s endless love is as vast as the sea. As kids sing and dance to this vibrant worship song, they’ll build lasting memories and grow stronger in their faith.

The Breaker Rock Beach VBS theme creates the perfect environment for kids to experience God’s presence through music. Just as waves continuously roll onto the shore, this theme song reinforces how God’s love is constant and never-ending. Parents and teachers will love seeing their children embrace worship with enthusiasm while learning fundamental Christian principles through memorable lyrics and motions.

Scripture

“But let all who take refuge in you be glad; let them ever sing for joy.” - Psalm 5:11

“The Lord your God is with you, the Mighty Warrior who saves. He will take great delight in you; in his love he will no longer rebuke you, but will rejoice over you with singing.” - Zephaniah 3:17

Worship Ideas

  • “Create wave motions with arms while singing the chorus”
  • “Use blue streamers or ribbons to simulate ocean waves”
  • “Form a “human wave” where kids stand in a line and do the wave motion”
  • “Add beach-themed percussion with seashell shakers or sand bottles”
  • “Create a simple line dance that mimics swimming or surfing moves”

Devotional Thought

Just like the ocean is full of endless waves, God’s love for us never runs dry. When we’re at the beach, we can see, hear, and feel the power of the waves - that’s how God’s love surrounds us too! He created the mighty ocean and every creature in it, and He created you with the same amazing care and attention.

Dear God, thank you for loving us as big as the ocean. Help us remember your love when we sing and dance to worship you. Amen.

FAQs

What age group is the Breaker Rock Beach theme song best suited for? The song is designed for children ages 4-12, with simple enough lyrics for preschoolers to follow along and engaging enough content to keep older elementary students interested.

How can I teach the motions to a large group of kids? Break down the movements into small sections, teaching one part at a time. Use the “mirror method” where you face the kids and demonstrate the motions slowly, then gradually increase speed as they learn.

Where can I find the sheet music and accompaniment tracks?
