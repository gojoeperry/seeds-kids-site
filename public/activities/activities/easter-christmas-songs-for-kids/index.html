Easter and Christmas Songs for Kids: Celebrating Jesus All Year Long

Introduction

Teaching children easter christmas songs for kids helps build a strong foundation of faith that can last a lifetime. These special seasonal songs connect young hearts to the two most significant events in Christianity - Jesus’s birth and resurrection. When kids learn and sing easter christmas songs for kids, they begin to understand the complete story of God’s love, from the manger to the empty tomb. These melodies create precious memories while teaching biblical truths in an engaging way that children naturally absorb and remember. By incorporating both Easter and Christmas music throughout the year, we help children grasp that Jesus’s whole story - from His humble birth to His triumphant resurrection - is worth celebrating in every season.

Scripture

“Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.” - Matthew 19:14

“Sing to the Lord a new song; sing to the Lord, all the earth.” - Psalm 96:1

Worship Ideas

  1. “Journey Through Jesus’s Story” Medley
  • “Start with “Away in a Manger” and add simple hand motions”
  • “Transition to “Jesus Loves Me”
  • “End with “He’s Alive” with jumping and celebrating”
  1. “Birthday Party for Jesus” Celebration
  • “Combine “Happy Birthday Jesus” with Easter praise songs”
  • “Use rhythm instruments during worship”
  • “Create a paper chain calendar connecting Christmas to Easter”
  1. “Musical Bible Story Time”
  • “Use songs to tell Jesus’s story from birth through resurrection”
  • “Include interactive movements and hand motions”
  • “Encourage children to act out different parts of the story”

Devotional Thought

Jesus came as a tiny baby because He loves us so much. He grew up, taught people about God’s love, died on the cross, and rose again - all to show how special we are to Him. When we sing songs about both Christmas and Easter, we remember this amazing story of love.

Dear Jesus, thank you for being our Savior from beginning to end. Help us remember your whole story as we sing and celebrate you. Amen.

FAQs

  1. What age group are these songs most appropriate for? These songs are generally best suited for children ages 2-10, but can be adapted for any age. Younger children enjoy the motions and simple melodies, while older kids can understand deeper meanings in the lyrics.

  2. How can I incorporate both Easter and Christmas songs throughout the year? Create themed worship times that tell Jesus’s complete story. Use seasonal songs during regular devotional times, Sunday School lessons, or bedtime routines. Connect the events through crafts, stories, and music.

  3. Where can I find quality Easter and Christmas songs for children? Look for resources from Christian children’s music publishers like Lifeway Kids, Group Publishing, or Seeds Family Worship. Many churches also offer children’s worship albums, and streaming platforms have dedicated Christian kids’ music channels.
