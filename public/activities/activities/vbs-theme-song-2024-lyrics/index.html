FAQs

What age group is the 2024 VBS theme song best suited for? The song is designed for children ages 4-12, with simple enough lyrics for preschoolers to follow along and engaging enough content to keep older elementary students interested.

How can I help my child learn the VBS theme song before summer? Start playing the song during car rides, incorporate it into bedtime routines, and practice small sections at a time. Many VBS programs also offer practice videos online that show the motions.

Where can I find resources for teaching the VBS theme song? Most VBS curriculum providers offer downloadable music tracks, lyric sheets, and motion videos through their websites. Your church’s children’s ministry director can also provide access to these materials.
