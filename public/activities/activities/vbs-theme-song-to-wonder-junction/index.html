Worship Ideas

  • “Create a “Wonder Wall” where kids can add colorful sticky notes with things they wonder about God while singing the theme song”
  • “Incorporate simple hand motions that reflect key words in the song (reaching up for “heaven,” making heart shapes for “love,” etc.)”
  • “Use rhythm instruments like tambourines and hand drums during the chorus to enhance engagement”
  • “Form a “Wonder Circle” where kids take turns leading different parts of the song from the center”

Devotional Thought

Just as God created all the wonderful things we see around us, He also gave us the gift of music to praise Him. When we sing the Wonder Junction songs, we’re joining with all of creation in celebrating God’s amazing love. Every time we lift our voices, we’re telling God how much we love Him and thanking Him for all He’s done.

Prayer: “Dear God, thank you for giving us music to praise you. Help us remember that our songs make you happy. We love you! Amen.”

FAQs

What age group is the Wonder Junction theme song best suited for? The Wonder Junction theme song is designed for children ages 4-12, with movements and lyrics that can be adapted for different age groups. Younger children especially enjoy the motions, while older kids can help lead and learn harmony parts.

How can I help my child practice the song at home? Create a special worship time during family devotions, use the song during car rides, or make it part of bedtime routines. You can also access online videos and resources to help learn the motions and lyrics together.

Where can I find the Wonder Junction theme song materials? The complete Wonder Junction theme song package, including music tracks, lyric sheets, and motion guides, is available through your church’s VBS curriculum provider or their official website. Many churches also offer take-home materials for families to continue worship at home.
