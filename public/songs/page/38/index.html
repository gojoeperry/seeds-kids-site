Fun Kids Memory Verse Songs: Making Scripture Stick Through Music

Introduction

Teaching children God’s Word through fun kids memory verse songs is one of the most effective ways to plant biblical truth in young hearts. As parents and teachers, we have the amazing opportunity to help children hide God’s Word in their hearts during these formative years. Music activates multiple areas of the brain, meaning those Scripture-based lyrics will stick with children long after the singing ends.
