Worship Ideas

  1. “Jesus Loves Me” with Sign Language: Teach simple hand motions while singing this classic tune. Make a heart shape for “loves,” point up for “Jesus,” and point to yourself for “me.”

  2. “B-I-B-L-E” Action Song: Have children hold their hands like an open book, then jump or march while spelling out “BIBLE” with their bodies.

  3. “Deep and Wide” with Movement: Use arm motions to show “deep” (reaching down) and “wide” (spreading arms), making it interactive and fun.

FAQs

What age is best to start using bible songs for preschoolers videos?

Children as young as 18 months can begin enjoying simple bible songs with videos. The ideal age range is 2-5 years old, when children are developing language skills and can follow basic instructions for movements and actions.

How can I incorporate these videos into our daily routine?

Try starting your day with a worship video during breakfast, use them during transition times, or make them part of your bedtime routine. Keep sessions short (5-10 minutes) and participate enthusiastically with your child to maximize engagement.

Where can I find quality bible songs for preschoolers videos?

Look for trusted Christian channels on YouTube like Bible Songs for Kids, Lifetree Kids, or Seeds Family Worship. Many Christian streaming services also offer curated collections of preschool worship videos. Your local Christian bookstore may carry DVDs with bible songs specifically designed for preschoolers.
