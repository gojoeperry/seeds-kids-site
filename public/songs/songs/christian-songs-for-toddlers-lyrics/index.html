FAQs

What age should I start teaching Christian songs to my toddler?

You can start as early as 12-18 months. At this age, children naturally respond to music and can begin mimicking simple words and motions. Even if they can’t sing all the words, they’ll absorb the melody and message.

How can I make worship songs more engaging for my toddler?

Incorporate movement, use visual aids like puppets or pictures, and repeat songs frequently. Make it interactive by adding clapping, dancing, or simple instruments. Keep sessions short (5-10 minutes) and energetic.
