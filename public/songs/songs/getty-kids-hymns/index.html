Worship Ideas

  1. Create hand motions for “The Perfect Wisdom of Our God” - let kids design movements for key phrases
  2. Use rhythm instruments while singing “Christ Our Hope in Life and Death” to keep young children engaged
  3. Make a worship chain - write one line from a Getty kids hymn on each paper link, connecting them as you learn the song

FAQs

What age group are Getty kids hymns best suited for? Getty kids hymns are generally appropriate for children ages 4-12, though younger children can enjoy the melodies and simple choruses. The songs grow with children as they mature in their understanding.

How can I incorporate Getty kids hymns into our daily routine? Try playing Getty kids hymns during breakfast, car rides, or bedtime routines. Make it interactive by adding motions, taking turns singing verses, or discussing the lyrics during family devotion time.
