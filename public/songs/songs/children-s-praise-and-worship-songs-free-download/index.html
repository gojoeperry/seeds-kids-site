Worship Ideas

  1. “Jesus Loves Me” with Sign Language Have children learn simple sign language motions while singing this classic. This combines worship with learning and physical movement.

  2. “This Little Light of Mine” Action Song Kids hold up their “light” (pointer finger) and follow actions like hiding it under a bush (other hand), letting it shine all around (make circles), and not letting Satan blow it out (shake finger “no”).

  3. “God’s Love is So Big” with Stretching Have children stretch their arms wide, high, and low to show how big, tall, and deep God’s love is.
