Action Songs For Kids English

Action Songs for Kids English: Making Worship Fun and Meaningful

Introduction

Action songs for kids in English are a powerful way to engage children in worship while helping them learn biblical truths through movement and music. These interactive worship songs combine simple melodies with hand motions and body movements, making it easier for young hearts to connect with God. When children participate in action songs for kids English, they’re not just learning lyrics – they’re experiencing the joy of praise with their whole being. This multi-sensory approach to worship helps children remember scripture verses, biblical concepts, and God’s love in a way that speaks their language. As parents and teachers, incorporating these engaging worship elements creates lasting spiritual memories and helps establish a foundation of faith that can grow throughout their lives.

Scripture

“Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.” - Psalm 98:4

“Let everything that has breath praise the Lord. Praise the Lord!” - Psalm 150:6

Worship Ideas

  1. “Jesus’ Love is Bubbling Over”
  • “Jump up and down during “bubbling over”
  • “Make wave motions for “flowing like a river”
  • “Point upward for “Jesus” and cross arms over heart for “love”
  1. “This Little Light of Mine”
  • “Hold up index finger as the “light”
  • “Make hiding motions under cupped hands”
  • “Circle motions for “let it shine all around the neighborhood”
  1. “Father Abraham”
  • “March in place while singing”
  • “Add movements progressively (right arm, left arm, right foot, left foot)”
  • “Turn around for the finale”

Devotional Thought

Just as our bodies can move and dance, our hearts can also move with love for Jesus. When we sing and dance in worship, we’re showing God that we love Him with everything we have – our voices, our movements, and our hearts. Jesus loves to see His children worship with joy and enthusiasm!

Prayer: “Dear Jesus, thank you for giving us songs to sing and ways to move as we praise You. Help us remember that worship can be fun and full of joy! Amen.”

FAQs

What age group is best for action songs in worship?

Action songs are most effective for children ages 2-10, but can be adapted for any age. Younger children (2-5) typically enjoy simple movements, while older kids (6-10) can handle more complex actions and longer songs.

How can I teach new action songs to my children?

Start by teaching the actions without the music, then add the words slowly. Break down complex movements into simple steps. Practice one verse at a time, and use repetition. Make it fun and don’t worry about perfection – enthusiasm is more important than accuracy!

Where can I find good action songs for kids worship?

You can find action songs through Christian children’s ministries like Hillsong Kids, VeggieTales, or Seeds Family Worship. Many churches also offer children’s worship resources, and YouTube channels dedicated to Christian kids’ music provide both songs and motion tutorials.
