Kids Faith Songs Videos: Growing Little Hearts Through Worship

Introduction

Teaching children about faith through engaging kids faith songs videos is one of the most powerful ways to plant seeds of spiritual truth in young hearts. These dynamic worship resources combine catchy melodies, biblical messages, and visual elements that capture children’s attention while nurturing their relationship with Jesus. When kids engage with faith-focused music and videos, they’re not just being entertained – they’re absorbing foundational truths about God’s love and character in ways that stick with them. Parents and teachers can use kids faith songs videos as tools to make spiritual education fun, memorable, and meaningful. This multi-sensory approach to worship helps children express their faith naturally while building a strong spiritual foundation.

Scripture

“Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.” - Matthew 19:14

“Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.” - Psalm 98:4

Worship Ideas

  1. “Jesus Loves Me” with Sign Language Have children learn basic sign language motions while singing this beloved hymn. This helps engage both mind and body in worship.

  2. “This Little Light of Mine” Action Song Kids hold up finger “candles” and act out letting their light shine, hiding it under a bushel (no!), and not letting Satan blow it out.

  3. “Bible Heroes Dance Party” Create simple choreography to songs about Bible characters, helping kids remember stories while moving and praising.

Devotional Thought

Just as Jesus welcomed children with open arms, He delights in hearing their voices raised in praise today. When we sing and dance for Jesus, we’re showing Him our love and joy. He doesn’t expect perfect performances – He loves our heartfelt worship!

Prayer: “Dear Jesus, thank you for loving us and giving us songs to sing for you. Help us remember that you’re always listening to our praise. Amen.”

FAQs

What age should children start watching worship videos?

Children as young as 18 months can benefit from age-appropriate worship videos. Look for content with simple lyrics, bright visuals, and basic movements. As children grow, gradually introduce more complex songs and biblical concepts.

How can I incorporate faith songs videos into our daily routine?

Try starting the day with a worship video during breakfast, using them during family devotion time, or making them part of bedtime routine. Keep sessions short (5-10 minutes) and interactive by joining in with your children.

Where can I find quality kids faith songs videos?

Many trusted Christian channels offer free content on YouTube, including Hillsong Kids, Seeds Family Worship, and Go Fish Kids. Christian streaming services like RightNow Media and JellyTelly also provide curated worship content for children. Your local Christian bookstore may carry DVD collections as well.
