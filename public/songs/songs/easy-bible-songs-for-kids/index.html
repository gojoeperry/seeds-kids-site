FAQs

What age should children start learning Bible songs?

Children can start learning Bible songs as early as infancy! Even babies respond to gentle melodies, and toddlers naturally enjoy music and movement. Start with very simple songs for young children and gradually introduce more complex ones as they grow.

How can I teach Bible songs to wiggly kids who can’t sit still?

Embrace the movement! Incorporate actions, hand motions, and dance moves into your songs. Let children march, jump, or act out the lyrics. Movement actually helps children remember the words better and keeps them engaged.
