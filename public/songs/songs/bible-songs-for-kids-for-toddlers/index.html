FAQs

What age should children start learning Bible songs?

Children can start learning Bible songs from birth! Even infants benefit from hearing sacred music, and toddlers as young as 18 months can begin participating with simple motions and words. The key is choosing age-appropriate songs with repetitive phrases and easy movements.

How can I teach Bible songs when I’m not musical?

Don’t worry about being perfectly in tune! Use YouTube videos, Christian children’s CDs, or streaming services to help lead the songs. Focus on enthusiasm and engagement rather than musical perfection. Children respond more to your joy and participation than your singing ability.
