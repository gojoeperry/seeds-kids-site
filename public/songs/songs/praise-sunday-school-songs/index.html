Praise Sunday School Songs

Teaching Kids to Worship Through Praise Sunday School Songs

Introduction

Praise Sunday school songs are a powerful way to help children develop a heart of worship and connect with God from an early age. These uplifting songs combine simple melodies with biblical truths, making them perfect for young hearts and minds. When children learn praise Sunday school songs, they’re not just memorizing words - they’re building a foundation of faith that can last a lifetime. Through these songs, kids discover the joy of expressing their love for God while learning essential spiritual truths in an age-appropriate way. Music has a unique ability to embed biblical principles deep in children’s hearts, and praise songs specifically designed for Sunday school create an atmosphere of joy and celebration while teaching about God’s character and love.

Scripture

“Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.” - Psalm 98:4

“Let everything that has breath praise the Lord. Praise the Lord!” - Psalm 150:6

Worship Ideas

  1. “Jesus Loves Me” with Actions
  • “Add simple hand motions for “loves me”
  • “March in place during chorus”
  • “Create a heart shape with hands”
  1. “This Little Light of Mine”
  • “Hold up finger “candles”
  • “Turn in circles for “let it shine”
  • “Point to others during “won’t let Satan blow it out”
  1. “Lord I Lift Your Name on High”
  • “Use full body motions to show “from the earth to the cross”
  • “Jump during “Lord I lift Your name on high”

Devotional Thought

God loves to hear our voices raised in praise! Just like parents love to hear their children say “I love you,” our Heavenly Father delights in our worship. When we sing praise songs, we’re telling God how much we love Him and thanking Him for His goodness. Every time we sing, we can remember that God is smiling, listening to our voices joined together in praise.

Prayer: “Dear God, thank you for giving us songs to sing. Help us to praise You with joy in our hearts. Amen.”

FAQs

What age should children start learning praise Sunday school songs?

Children can start learning praise songs as early as age 2-3. Even toddlers can clap, move to the music, and begin learning simple phrases. As they grow older, they can understand more complex lyrics and concepts.

How can I make praise songs more engaging for kids?

Incorporate hand motions, body movements, and simple instruments like tambourines or shakers. Use visual aids like pictures or puppets, and encourage children to create their own motions. Make it interactive by having them take turns leading or suggesting actions.

Where can I find good praise Sunday school songs to teach?

Many Christian children’s ministry websites offer free song resources. Popular sources include Kids Worship Songs on YouTube, Christian children’s music publishers like Lifeway Kids, and Sunday school curriculum providers. Local Christian bookstores often carry children’s worship CDs and DVDs.
