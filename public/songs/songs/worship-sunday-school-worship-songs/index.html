FAQs

What age should children start learning worship songs? Children can start learning worship songs as early as age 2-3. Even babies can benefit from hearing worship music! Start with simple songs and add more complex ones as they grow.

How can I make worship time more engaging for fidgety kids? Include movement and actions with the songs, use visual aids like pictures or puppets, and keep sessions short (10-15 minutes). Alternate between fast and slow songs to maintain interest.

Where can I find good worship resources for kids? Many Christian publishers offer children’s worship curriculum, including Hillsong Kids, Orange, and Group Publishing. YouTube channels like KidsWorship.com provide free resources. Your local Christian bookstore likely has children’s worship CDs and DVDs.
