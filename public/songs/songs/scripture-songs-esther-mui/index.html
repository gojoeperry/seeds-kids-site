Worship Ideas

  1. Create hand motions to accompany Esther Mui’s Scripture songs. Let children help design movements that match the words.
  2. Make a Scripture song calendar, featuring one new song each week for memorization.
  3. Play “Scripture Song Freeze” - dance while the music plays, freeze when it stops, and try to continue singing the verse.

FAQs

What age group are Esther Mui’s Scripture songs best suited for? While these songs are appropriate for all ages, they work especially well for children ages 4-12. The melodies are simple enough for preschoolers to follow, while the content remains meaningful for older children.

How can I incorporate these songs into our daily routine? Try playing them during car rides, morning devotions, or bedtime routines. You can also use them during chore time or as transition music between activities. Consistency helps children learn the verses naturally.

Where can I find Esther Mui’s Scripture songs? Esther Mui’s Scripture songs are available on YouTube, her official website, and various streaming platforms. Many are free to access, and you can also purchase albums or download individual tracks for offline use.
