One Voice Children’s Choir “I’ll Always Remember You”: A Guide for Young Worship

Introduction

The One Voice Children’s Choir’s rendition of “I’ll Always Remember You” has touched hearts worldwide, showcasing how young voices can powerfully express God’s love through music. This inspiring performance by the One Voice Children’s Choir demonstrates that children have a unique ability to worship with pure hearts and genuine emotion. When kids participate in worship through songs like “I’ll Always Remember You” by One Voice Children’s Choir, they develop a deeper understanding of God’s faithfulness and His constant presence in their lives. This musical experience helps children express their faith while building confidence in their spiritual journey. For parents and teachers, it’s a beautiful opportunity to nurture young hearts in worship and help them understand that their voices matter in God’s kingdom.

Scripture

“Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.” - Matthew 19:14

“Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.” - Psalm 98:4

Worship Ideas

  1. Create a “Remember God’s Love” craft where children write memories of God’s faithfulness on paper hearts while listening to the song.
  2. Teach simple hand motions that match the lyrics, helping children connect physically with the message.
  3. Form a small choir group where children can learn harmonies and experience the joy of singing together.

Devotional Thought

Just as we remember special moments with friends and family, God always remembers us. He never forgets His children, and His love stays with us forever. When we sing worship songs, we’re creating beautiful memories with God that strengthen our relationship with Him.

Dear God, thank you for always remembering us and loving us. Help us to remember You in everything we do and sing. Amen.

FAQs

Q: What age group is best suited for learning “I’ll Always Remember You”? A: This song is particularly suitable for children aged 7-14, though younger children can also participate with simplified parts. The message resonates with all ages, making it perfect for family worship.

Q: How can I help my child connect with the song’s message? A: Share personal stories of God’s faithfulness, encourage them to journal their own experiences with God, and discuss how the lyrics relate to their daily lives. Make it interactive by creating artwork or movements to express the song’s meaning.

Q: Where can I find resources for teaching this song to children? A: You can find sheet music, backing tracks, and teaching materials on the One Voice Children’s Choir’s official website, YouTube channel, and various Christian music education platforms. Many churches also offer children’s choir programs that include similar worship songs.
