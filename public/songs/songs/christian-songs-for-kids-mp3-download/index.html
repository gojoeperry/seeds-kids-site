Worship Ideas

  1. “Jesus Loves Me” Action Time: Download this classic and add hand motions - cross over heart for “loves,” point up for “Jesus,” point to self for “me”

  2. “Bible Heroes Sing-Along”: Create a musical story time with songs about David, Daniel, and Moses. Let kids act out the stories while singing.

  3. “Praise Party Dance”: Download upbeat worship songs and create simple choreography that kids can follow, like jumping during the chorus or spinning when they hear specific words.
