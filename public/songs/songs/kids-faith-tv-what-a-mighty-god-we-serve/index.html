Kids Faith TV What a Mighty God we Serve

Introduction

Welcome to an exciting journey of worship with “Kids Faith TV What a Mighty God we serve” - a special way to help children experience the amazing power and love of our Creator! This engaging worship experience helps young hearts connect with God through music, movement, and meaningful moments of praise. When kids learn to worship through “Kids Faith TV What a Mighty God we serve,” they develop a foundational understanding of God’s greatness while having fun and building their faith. This program combines dynamic worship songs, biblical truths, and interactive elements that make praising God natural and enjoyable for children. By introducing kids to worship early, we help them build a lifelong relationship with Jesus and understand just how mighty and loving our God truly is.

Scripture

“Great is the Lord and most worthy of praise; his greatness no one can fathom.” - Psalm 145:3

“Let everything that has breath praise the Lord. Praise the Lord!” - Psalm 150:6

Worship Ideas

  1. “Mighty God Dance”: Create simple hand motions showing God’s power (raising arms for mountains, wave movements for oceans, twirling for stars). Sing about God’s mighty works while doing the motions.

  2. “Creation Celebration”: Have children act out different parts of creation (animals, trees, stars) while singing about God’s mighty power in creating everything.

  3. “Thunder Claps of Praise”: Lead kids in rhythmic clapping patterns while shouting praise phrases like “God is mighty!” “God is strong!” “God is amazing!”

Devotional Thought

God is bigger than our biggest problems and stronger than the strongest thing we can imagine! Just like a loving parent who can pick up their child, God’s mighty power comes with gentle love. He uses His strength to protect us, help us, and show us how much He cares.

Prayer: “Dear God, thank you for being so mighty and strong. Thank you for using your power to take care of us. Help us remember how amazing you are! Amen.”

FAQs

What age group is Kids Faith TV What a Mighty God we serve best suited for?

This worship program is designed for children ages 4-11, with content that can be adapted for different age groups. Younger children enjoy the movements and songs, while older kids can engage with deeper spiritual concepts about God’s power and presence.

How can I incorporate these worship activities at home?

Start with just 10-15 minutes during family devotion time. Use simple props from around the house, play worship music, and encourage everyone to participate. Make it a regular part of your routine, perhaps before bedtime or during breakfast.

Where can I find more resources for kids’ worship?

You can find additional materials through Christian bookstores, church resource centers, and online platforms like RightNow Media or YouTube Kids’ Christian channels. Many churches also offer children’s ministry curriculum that includes worship elements you can use at home.
