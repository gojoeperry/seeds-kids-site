The Bible Tells Me So Song Lyrics: Teaching Children God’s Love

Introduction

Teaching children about God’s love through music is one of the most powerful ways to nurture their faith. “The Bible tells me so song lyrics” have been helping young hearts understand God’s truth for generations. When children learn these simple yet profound words, they begin to grasp the fundamental message that Jesus loves them unconditionally. The melody and movements make spiritual truths memorable, while the repetitive nature of “The Bible tells me so song lyrics” helps reinforce these essential messages of faith. For parents and teachers, these songs provide an engaging way to introduce biblical concepts during worship time, bedtime routines, or Sunday school activities.

Scripture

“Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.” - Matthew 19:14

“I have loved you with an everlasting love.” - Jeremiah 31:3

Worship Ideas

Create an interactive worship experience by incorporating simple hand motions:

  • “Point upward when referencing Jesus or heaven”
  • “Cross arms over heart when singing about love”
  • “Open hands like a book when mentioning the Bible”
  • “Form a circle and hold hands while singing”
  • “Use rhythm instruments like tambourines or bells”
  • “Create colorful worship flags to wave during music time”

Devotional Thought

God’s love is like a big, warm hug that never ends. Just as parents love their children no matter what, God’s love for us is even bigger and stronger. The Bible is like God’s special letter to us, telling us again and again how much He cares for each one of us.

Prayer: “Dear Jesus, thank you for loving me. Help me remember that your love is always with me, just like the song says. Amen.”

FAQs

Q: What age group is this song most appropriate for? A: This song is ideal for children ages 2-8, though older children also benefit from its simple yet profound message. The tune and movements are easy enough for toddlers, while the message remains meaningful for elementary-aged children.

Q: How can I incorporate this song into our daily routine? A: Try singing it during morning devotions, as part of bedtime prayers, or during car rides. Make it special by creating a consistent time when your family sings it together, perhaps adding personal verses or special movements.

Q: Where can I find resources for teaching this song? A: Many children’s ministry websites offer free teaching resources, including motion tutorials and lesson plans. Your local Christian bookstore likely carries CDs or DVDs featuring this and other children’s worship songs. Church children’s ministers can also provide additional resources and teaching tips.
