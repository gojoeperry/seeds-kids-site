Cedarmont Kids Hymns Songs: Teaching Children Timeless Worship

Introduction

Cedarmont Kids hymns songs have been helping children connect with God through music for generations. These beloved collections combine traditional hymns with child-friendly arrangements, making timeless truths accessible to young hearts. When children learn these foundational worship songs early, they build a strong spiritual foundation that can last a lifetime. The simple melodies and clear lyrics of Cedarmont Kids hymns songs help youngsters understand biblical concepts while creating joyful memories of praising God. Parents and teachers appreciate how these songs make it easy to introduce children to the rich heritage of Christian worship while keeping them engaged and excited about their faith journey.

Scripture

“Let everything that has breath praise the Lord. Praise the Lord!” (Psalm 150:6)

“Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.” (Psalm 98:4)

Worship Ideas

  1. “Jesus Loves Me” Action Time
  • “Have children cross arms over chest during “Jesus loves me”
  • “Point upward for “this I know”
  • “Hold up Bible for “for the Bible tells me so”
  1. “This Little Light of Mine” Circle Activity
  • “Form a circle with children holding battery-operated tea lights”
  • “Raise lights high during “let it shine”
  • “Hide lights behind backs for “hide it under a bushel? No!”
  1. “He’s Got the Whole World” Movement Song
  • “Use hand motions to form a circle for “whole world”
  • “Rock arms like holding a baby for “little bitty babies”
  • “Point up for “He’s got you and me”

Devotional Thought

God gives us songs to help us remember His love and goodness. Just like how you might remember the ABC song to learn your letters, worship songs help us remember important things about God. When we sing hymns together, we’re telling God how much we love Him and reminding ourselves of His promises to us.

Dear God, thank you for giving us music to praise You. Help us use our voices to share Your love with others. Amen.

FAQs

Q: What age group are Cedarmont Kids hymns songs best suited for? A: These songs are primarily designed for children ages 2-10, but they can be enjoyed by the whole family. The simple melodies and repetitive lyrics make them especially effective for preschool and early elementary ages.

Q: How can I incorporate these songs into our daily routine? A: Try playing them during car rides, morning devotion time, or before bedtime prayers. You can also use them during family worship time or while doing household chores to make everyday activities more spiritually meaningful.

Q: Where can I find Cedarmont Kids hymns songs resources? A: These songs are available on multiple platforms including YouTube, Spotify, and Amazon Music. You can also purchase DVDs and CDs directly from Christian bookstores or online retailers. Many churches also include these songs in their children’s ministry curriculum materials.
