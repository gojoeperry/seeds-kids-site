Just like the first volume, these scripture songs create an enjoyable way for kids to learn and remember biblical truths through the power of music. Scripture

“Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly, teaching and admonishing one another in all wisdom, singing psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, with thankfulness in your hearts to God.” - Colossians 3:16

“Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path.” - Psalm 119:105

Worship Ideas

  1. Bible Verse Bounce: Have children bounce a ball while singing each word of the verse, helping them remember the rhythm and words.
  2. Scripture Sign Language: Teach simple hand motions for key words in each verse-song, making it more interactive and memorable.
  3. Musical Memory Circle: Form a circle and pass a Bible while singing the scripture song, stopping the music randomly for children to recite the verse.

Devotional Thought

God’s Word is like a beautiful song in our hearts that helps us remember His love and truth. When Jesus was on earth, He often quoted scripture because He knew how important God’s Word is. When we learn these scripture songs, we’re following Jesus’s example and filling our minds with good things that help us grow closer to God.

Dear God, thank you for giving us Your Word and helping us learn it through music. Help us to remember these songs and live by Your truth. Amen.

Q: How can I incorporate these songs into our daily routine? A: Play them during car rides, morning devotions, or bedtime routines. You can also use them during household chores or as transition songs between activities.
