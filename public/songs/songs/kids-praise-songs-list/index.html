Kids Praise Songs List

Best Kids Praise Songs List for Joyful Worship

Introduction

Creating a meaningful kids praise songs list is essential for helping children develop a lifelong love of worship. When young hearts connect with God through music, they learn valuable truths about His character while having fun. The right kids praise songs list can transform ordinary moments into sacred opportunities for spiritual growth, whether at home, church, or Sunday school. Modern worship songs for children combine catchy melodies with biblical truths, making it easier for kids to remember and internalize God’s Word. As parents and teachers, introducing children to praise music early helps establish a foundation of faith that can last a lifetime.

Scripture

“Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.” - Psalm 98:4

“Let everything that has breath praise the Lord. Praise the Lord!” - Psalm 150:6

Worship Ideas

  1. “Every Move I Make” - Have children form a circle and follow simple motions: jumping, clapping, spinning while singing about God’s love.

  2. “Jesus Loves Me Rock” - Add hand motions and body movements to this classic, letting kids create their own praise choreography.

  3. “This Little Light of Mine” - Give each child a battery-operated tea light to hold while singing, teaching them about sharing God’s love.

Devotional Thought

Just as birds sing their beautiful songs each morning, God created us to praise Him with joy. When we worship, we’re telling God how much we love Him and thanking Him for His goodness. Jesus loves to hear children’s voices lifted in praise - remember how He welcomed the little children and said the kingdom of heaven belongs to them?

Dear God, thank you for giving us voices to sing and hearts to love You. Help us praise You with joy every day. Amen.

FAQs

What age should children start learning praise songs?

Children can start learning praise songs as early as age 2-3. Even toddlers naturally respond to music and can begin learning simple songs with basic motions. As they grow, they can graduate to more complex songs and deeper meanings.

How can I make praise time more engaging for kids?

Make worship interactive by adding hand motions, instruments, or props. Create a special worship corner in your home with instruments and song sheets. Encourage children to dance, clap, and move freely while praising God. The key is making it fun while maintaining reverence.

Where can I find good kids worship resources?

Look for kids worship videos on YouTube channels like Hillsong Kids, GoFish, or Seeds Family Worship. Many churches also offer children’s worship albums and resources. Christian bookstores and websites like Planning Center or WorshipTogether often have dedicated children’s sections with downloadable materials.
