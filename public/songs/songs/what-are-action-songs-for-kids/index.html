What Are Action Songs for Kids?

Introduction

Action songs for kids are energetic worship songs that combine music with physical movements, helping children engage with God in a fun and memorable way. When exploring what are action songs for kids, parents and teachers discover powerful tools for spiritual formation. These interactive songs allow children to worship with their whole bodies while learning biblical truths through repetition and movement. Action songs create an atmosphere where kids can express their love for Jesus naturally and joyfully, making worship accessible and enjoyable. By incorporating motion with music, children retain spiritual lessons better and develop a positive association with church and worship time. Understanding what are action songs for kids helps us nurture the next generation’s faith journey through age-appropriate, engaging worship experiences.

Scripture

“Let them praise his name with dancing and make music to him with timbrel and harp.” - Psalm 149:3

“Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.” - Psalm 98:4

Worship Ideas

  1. “Jesus’ Love is Bubbling Over” - Children pretend to be bubbles, jumping up and down while singing about Jesus’ love overflowing in their hearts.

  2. “This Little Light of Mine” - Kids hold up pointer fingers like candles, moving them in different directions while singing about sharing God’s light.

  3. “Father Abraham” - A classic action song where children perform different movements (right arm, left arm, right foot, etc.) while singing about being children of faith.

Devotional Thought

Just as our bodies move and dance when we’re happy, God loves to see us express our joy in worship. When we sing and move in praise to Him, we’re using the amazing bodies He gave us to show Him how much we love Him. Jesus welcomed children to come to Him, and He delights in their energetic worship.

Prayer: “Dear Jesus, thank you for giving us songs to sing and ways to move as we worship You. Help us to always praise You with joy in our hearts. Amen.”

FAQs

What age group is best for action songs?

Action songs are most effective for children ages 2-10, but can be adapted for any age. Toddlers especially enjoy simple motions, while older children can handle more complex movements and lyrics.

How can I teach action songs effectively?

Break down the movements into simple steps, demonstrate them clearly, and practice slowly before adding music. Start with basic motions and gradually add complexity. Make it fun and don’t worry about perfection.

Where can I find good action songs for kids?

You can find action songs through Christian children’s ministry websites, YouTube channels like Kids Worship Songs, and children’s worship curriculum providers. Many churches also offer resources through their children’s ministry departments.
