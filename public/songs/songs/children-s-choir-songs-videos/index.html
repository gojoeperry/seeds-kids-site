Children’s Choir Songs Videos: Teaching Kids to Worship Through Music

Introduction

Children’s choir songs videos have become an invaluable resource for parents and ministry leaders seeking to nurture young hearts in worship. These engaging musical tools combine visual elements with sacred songs, making it easier than ever to teach children about God’s love through music. Whether used in Sunday School, children’s church, or at home, children’s choir songs videos provide a dynamic way to help kids express their faith joyfully. The combination of melody, movement, and biblical truth creates lasting impressions that can shape a child’s spiritual journey. By incorporating these videos into worship time, we give our children a foundation of praise that can grow with them throughout their lives.

Worship Ideas

  1. “The B-I-B-L-E” with Sign Language Have children learn simple sign language motions while singing this classic song. Each letter can be signed, making it both educational and fun.

  2. “This Little Light of Mine” Action Song Create a circle and have kids hold up their “light” (pointer finger) while singing. Add movements like hiding their light under a bush (other hand), and letting it shine all around (spinning).

  3. “Jesus Loves Me” Echo Version Split the group into two sections, with one group echoing each line. Add simple hand motions to reinforce the message.

Devotional Thought

Just as different instruments in an orchestra make beautiful music together, God made each of us unique but special. When we sing together in worship, it’s like we’re joining a heavenly choir! Our voices might be different, but they all blend together to praise God.

Dear God, thank you for giving us voices to sing your praise. Help us to worship you with joy and love. Amen.

FAQs

What age is appropriate for children’s choir videos?

Most children’s choir songs videos are suitable for ages 3-12, though the sweet spot is typically ages 4-8. Younger children enjoy the movements and repetition, while older kids can grasp deeper meanings in the lyrics.

How can I use choir videos effectively at home?

Set aside regular worship time where you watch and sing along with the videos together. Make it interactive by learning the motions first, then practicing without the video. Create a playlist of favorite songs and use them during daily routines or family devotional time.

Where can I find quality children’s choir songs videos?

Many Christian publishers offer dedicated children’s worship channels on YouTube. Popular sources include Lifeway Kids Worship, Hillsong Kids, and Seeds Family Worship. Local Christian bookstores often carry DVD collections, and many church curriculum providers include digital worship resources.
