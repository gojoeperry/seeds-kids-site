## Worship Ideas
1. "God's Love Is Like a Ocean" - Have children make wave motions with their arms while singing about God's endless love, gradually building from small waves to big ones.

2. "Jesus' Light Shines Through Me" - Give each child a battery-operated tea light and dim the room. As they sing about being God's light, they raise their lights high.

3. "Creation Celebration" - Create a progressive song where kids add animal sounds and movements for each day of creation, building to a grand finale.

### FAQs
**What age group is best suited for VBS music themes?**
VBS music themes typically work best for children ages 4-12, but can be adapted for both younger and older groups. The key is choosing songs with age-appropriate lyrics and movements that match their physical abilities.

**How can I teach new VBS songs effectively?**
Break songs into smaller parts, teach the motions first, then add words gradually. Use visual aids like lyric posters with pictures, and always model enthusiasm. Practice one section at a time before putting it all together.

**Where can I find quality VBS music resources?**
