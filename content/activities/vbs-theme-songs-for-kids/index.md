## Worship Ideas
1. "God's Love Is Like a Lighthouse" - Have children make wave motions with their arms during verses and form a lighthouse shape during the chorus.

2. "Jesus' Power Pulls Us Through" - Create a train formation where kids hold onto each other's shoulders and move around the room, demonstrating how Jesus leads us.

3. "Kingdom Kids" - Use simple sign language for key words (crown for kingdom, heart for love, hands raised for praise) while singing.

### FAQs

### What age group are VBS theme songs most appropriate for?
VBS theme songs are typically designed for children ages 4-12, but can be adapted for both younger and older kids. Preschoolers might focus on simple motions and choruses, while older children can learn more complex lyrics and harmonies.

### How can I help my children remember VBS songs throughout the year?
Create a playlist of VBS songs to play during car rides, incorporate them into daily devotional time, and use hand motions or dance moves from VBS during family worship. You can also make simple instruments or use the songs during bedtime routines.

### Where can I find quality VBS theme songs and resources?
Many Christian publishers offer complete VBS music packages, including LifeWay, Group Publishing, and Orange. You can also find VBS songs on Christian streaming platforms, YouTube channels dedicated to children's ministry, and through your local church's children's ministry resources.
