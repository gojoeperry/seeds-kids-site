## Worship Ideas
1. "Jesus Is Alive" - A bouncy song with simple motions (jumping on "alive," making cross shapes with arms)
2. "Easter Morning Celebration" - Use egg shakers or ribbons while singing about the empty tomb
3. "God's Love Is Like a Party" - Create a parade around the room with streamers and tambourines

Make these songs interactive by adding simple choreography or having children take turns leading motions. Consider creating visual aids like picture cards to help younger children follow along.
