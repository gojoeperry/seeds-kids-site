## Worship Ideas
1. "His Peace Like a River" - Have children sway gently while sitting, making wave motions with their arms. During the chorus, they can stand and make flowing river movements.

2. "Be Still and Know" - Create a quiet moment where children sit in a circle, holding battery-operated candles, and sing softly while taking turns sharing what makes them feel God's peace.

3. "The Peace of Christ" - Teach simple sign language for "peace" and "love," incorporating these gestures throughout the song.

### FAQs

### What age group are peaceful VBS theme songs best suited for?
Peaceful VBS theme songs work well for all ages, but they're especially effective for children ages 4-12. Younger children (4-7) connect well with simple, repetitive lyrics, while older kids (8-12) can appreciate deeper meanings and more complex arrangements.

### How can I help children engage with peaceful worship songs when they're feeling energetic?
Start with movement-based songs to expend energy, then gradually transition to peaceful songs. Use gentle motions, sign language, or quiet instruments to keep them engaged while creating a calming atmosphere. Consider having them close their eyes or use visualization techniques.

### Where can I find quality peaceful VBS theme songs?
You can find peaceful VBS songs through Christian music publishers like LifeWay and Group Publishing, streaming platforms like Spotify's Christian Kids playlists, or VBS curriculum providers. Many churches also share their resources through YouTube channels dedicated to children's ministry.
