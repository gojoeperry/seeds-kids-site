## Worship Ideas
1. "Star of Bethlehem Dance" - Have children hold star cutouts while twirling and moving to peaceful Christmas worship music. Each time the music pauses, they can freeze and point their stars toward "baby Jesus" (a designated spot).

2. "Birthday Party for Jesus" - Combine familiar Christmas carols with birthday party motions. During "Joy to the World," children can throw pretend confetti and celebrate Jesus's birth.

3. "Silent Night Actions" - Teach simple hand motions for each verse, helping children connect the story with gentle movements.
