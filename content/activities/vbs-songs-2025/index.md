## Worship Ideas
1. "God's Love Is Like a Lighthouse" - Have children make wave motions with their arms during verses and spin like a lighthouse beam during the chorus. Add colored streamers for extra fun!

2. "Jesus, My Superhero" - Kids can strike superhero poses while singing about God's mighty power. Include cape movements and "flying" actions during the bridge.

3. "Creation Celebration" - Create animal sounds and movements for each day of creation, building to a grand finale of praise.

### FAQs

### What age group are the VBS songs 2025 best suited for?
The new VBS songs 2025 collection is designed for children ages 4-12, with varying complexity levels to engage both preschoolers and older elementary students. The songs include simple versions for younger children and more complex arrangements for older kids.

### How can I teach these songs effectively to my group?
Start by breaking down the songs into smaller sections, teaching one part at a time. Use hand motions and visual aids to help kids remember lyrics. Practice regularly, and don't forget to explain what the words mean so children understand what they're singing about.
