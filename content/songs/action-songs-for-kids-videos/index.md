## Worship Ideas
1. "Every Move I Make" - Have children follow simple hand motions that point up to heaven during "every move," and spread arms wide during "in You." Add jumping and spinning movements during chorus.

2. "This Little Light of Mine" - Create hand motions for "light" (hold up pointer finger), "hide it under a bushel" (cover light with other hand), and "let it shine" (raise hands high and wiggle fingers).

3. "Jesus Loves Me" - Incorporate sign language for "Jesus," "loves," and "me" while singing this classic children's worship song.

### FAQs

**Q: What age group is best suited for action songs videos?**
A: Action songs videos are typically most effective for children ages 2-10, with different styles appropriate for different age ranges. Toddlers and preschoolers enjoy simple, repetitive movements, while older children can handle more complex actions and lyrics.

**Q: How can I incorporate action songs into our daily routine?**
A: Start by choosing a regular time, such as morning devotions or bedtime. Begin with one or two familiar songs, gradually adding new ones. Use action songs during transition times, car rides, or as energy breaks during the day.
