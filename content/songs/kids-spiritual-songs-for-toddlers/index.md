### FAQs
Q: What age should I start teaching spiritual songs to my child?
A: You can begin as early as infancy! Babies and toddlers naturally respond to music, and simple spiritual songs can become part of your daily routines like bedtime or mealtime prayers.

Q: How can I make worship songs more engaging for my toddler?
A: Incorporate movement, use props like stuffed animals or scarves, and keep sessions short (5-10 minutes). Repetition is key, and don't worry if your toddler doesn't sing every word perfectly.

Q: Where can I find age-appropriate worship songs for toddlers?
A: Look for Christian children's music on platforms like YouTube Kids, Christian streaming services, or your local Christian bookstore. Many churches also offer resources through their children's ministry programs.
