## Worship Ideas
1. "Jesus' Love is Bubbling Over" - Have kids pretend to be bubbles rising and popping while singing about God's overflowing love
2. "This Little Light of Mine" - Use phone flashlights or finger motions to show lights shining bright
3. "God's Army" - March in place, make trumpet sounds, and follow simple hand motions while learning about being strong in the Lord

### Devotional Thought
Just as our bodies move and dance when we're happy, God loves to see us express our joy in worship. Jesus welcomes children to come to Him with their whole hearts and bodies. When we sing and move in worship, we're showing God how much we love Him, just like David danced before the Lord with all his might.
