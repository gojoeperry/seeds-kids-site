## Biblical Foundation for Worship Music in Children's Lives

Scripture consistently emphasizes music's role in spiritual formation. Colossians 3:16 instructs us to "let the message of Christ dwell among you richly as you teach and admonish one another with all wisdom through psalms, hymns, and songs from the Spirit, singing to God with gratitude in your hearts."

This verse reveals music's dual purpose: teaching biblical truth and creating heart-level worship. When we follow Deuteronomy 6:6-7 to teach God's Word diligently to our children, music becomes a powerful vehicle for Scripture memorization and spiritual understanding.

Psalm 96:1 calls us to "sing to the Lord a new song," while Psalm 150:6 declares "let everything that has breath praise the Lord!" Children's voices joining in Scripture songs fulfill these biblical commands while building lasting faith foundations.
