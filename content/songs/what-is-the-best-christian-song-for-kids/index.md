# What Is The Best Christian Song For Kids?

# What is the Best Christian Song for Kids?
### Introduction
When asking "what is the best Christian song for kids?", parents and teachers often discover there isn't just one perfect answer – but rather a wonderful variety of songs that can help children grow in their faith. The best Christian songs for kids combine memorable melodies with biblical truth, making worship both fun and meaningful. These songs plant seeds of faith that can flourish throughout a child's life, helping them understand God's love in ways they can grasp and remember. Whether you're leading worship in Sunday School, planning family devotions, or simply wanting to fill your home with godly music, choosing the right Christian songs for kids is an important part of their spiritual development.

### Scripture
"Let everything that has breath praise the Lord. Praise the Lord!" (Psalm 150:6)

"Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise." (Psalm 98:4)

## Worship Ideas
1. "Jesus Loves Me" with sign language - Teach children to sign basic words like "Jesus," "loves," and "me" while singing this classic.

2. "Deep and Wide" with movement - Include traditional hand motions and encourage kids to create their own movements to express the lyrics.

3. "This Little Light of Mine" with finger lights - Give each child a small LED finger light to wave during the chorus, teaching about being God's light in the world.

### Devotional Thought
Just as birds sing their special songs each morning, God gave us voices to sing His praises. When we worship God through song, we're showing Him our love and thankfulness. Jesus loves to hear children's voices lifted in praise – remember how He welcomed the children and how they shouted "Hosanna!" in the temple?

Prayer: "Dear Jesus, thank you for giving us music and songs to praise You. Help us to sing with joy and to remember that You love hearing our voices. Amen."

### FAQs

### What age should children start learning worship songs?
Children can start learning simple worship songs as early as age 2-3. Even babies benefit from hearing Christian music! Start with short, repetitive songs and gradually introduce more complex ones as they grow.

### How can I make worship songs more engaging for my kids?
Add hand motions, use instruments (even homemade ones), create action cards, or incorporate dance moves. Visual aids like puppets or pictures can also help keep children engaged while learning new songs.

### Where can I find good Christian songs for kids?
Look for resources from trusted Christian publishers like Lifeway Kids, Seeds Family Worship, or Hillsong Kids. Many churches also have children's worship curricula available. Online platforms like YouTube Kids offer numerous Christian children's music channels with animated videos and lyrics.
