## Worship Ideas
1. "Mirror Movements": Have children face each other in pairs and take turns leading simple hand motions to their favorite One Voice songs, helping them engage physically with the music.

2. "Rainbow Ribbons": Use colorful ribbon wands during upbeat worship songs, encouraging children to create flowing movements that express joy and praise.

3. "Echo Singing": Break down One Voice songs into simple phrases that children can echo, helping them learn lyrics while building confidence in their voices.
