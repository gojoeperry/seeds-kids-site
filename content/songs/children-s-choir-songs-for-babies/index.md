### FAQs
Q: What age should I start introducing children's choir songs to my baby?
A: You can start from birth! Babies recognize and respond to music even in the womb. Simple, gentle songs create bonding opportunities and help develop both musical and spiritual awareness from the earliest days.

Q: How can I make choir songs engaging for babies who can't yet sing along?
A: Use gentle movements, facial expressions, and simple props. Incorporate soft touches, rocking motions, and visual elements like scarves or small instruments. Consistency and repetition help babies learn and engage with the music.

Q: Where can I find appropriate children's choir songs for babies?
A: Many Christian bookstores offer baby-friendly worship albums. Online resources like YouTube channels dedicated to Christian children's music, church websites, and baby worship apps are also excellent sources. Look for collections specifically designed for infants with simple melodies and clear, gentle vocals.
