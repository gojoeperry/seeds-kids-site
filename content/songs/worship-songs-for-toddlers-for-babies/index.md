### FAQs

### What age should I start introducing worship songs to my baby?
You can start from birth! Babies respond to music from their earliest days, and singing gentle worship songs during feeding, bedtime, or playtime creates positive associations with faith and music.

### How can I make worship songs more engaging for my active toddler?
Include lots of movement and actions with the songs. Use props like scarves, bells, or stuffed animals. Make it interactive by having them follow simple actions or repeat words. Keep sessions short and energetic.

### Where can I find age-appropriate worship songs for my little one?
Look for Christian children's music on streaming platforms, YouTube Kids channels focused on Christian content, or children's worship CDs from Christian bookstores. Many churches also have children's worship resources available for families.
