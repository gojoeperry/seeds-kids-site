# Kids Christian Music Videos: Growing Faith Through Song

### Introduction
Kids Christian music videos provide an engaging and memorable way for children to learn about God's love while having fun. These dynamic worship resources combine catchy tunes, biblical messages, and colorful visuals that capture young hearts and minds. When children experience kids Christian music videos, they're not just being entertained – they're building a foundation of faith through song, movement, and joy. This multi-sensory approach to worship helps Biblical truths stick in their memory and gives them a positive association with church and spiritual growth. Parents and teachers can use these videos as powerful tools to nurture their children's relationship with Jesus, making faith come alive through music and motion.

### Scripture
"Let everything that has breath praise the Lord!" (Psalm 150:6)

"Make a joyful noise to the Lord, all the earth! Serve the Lord with gladness! Come into his presence with singing!" (Psalm 100:1-2)

## Worship Ideas
1. "Bible Heroes Dance Party" - Create movements for different Bible characters (David's dance, Moses parting the sea, Joshua marching around Jericho)

2. "Jesus Loves Me Remix" - Add hand motions and simple choreography to this classic song, encouraging kids to make up their own moves

3. "Praise Parade" - Form a conga line and march around the room while singing worship songs, waving ribbons or scarves

### Devotional Thought
Just as music makes our hearts happy, praising God brings joy to His heart too! When we sing and dance for Jesus, we're showing Him how much we love Him. God gave us voices and bodies to worship Him in fun ways. Remember, you don't have to be the best singer or dancer – God loves hearing your voice and seeing your joy as you praise Him.

Dear God, thank you for music that helps us learn about you. Help us to praise you with all our heart. Amen.

### FAQs
**What age group is best for kids Christian music videos?**
Most kids Christian music videos are designed for children ages 2-10, but different series target specific age ranges. Preschoolers often enjoy simple, repetitive songs with basic movements, while older children appreciate more complex lyrics and choreography.

**How can I incorporate music videos into our family devotion time?**
Start with one video per devotion time, discussing the lyrics and message afterward. Make it interactive by learning the movements together, then use them throughout the week. You can also create a playlist of favorite worship videos for car rides or playtime.

**Where can I find quality Christian music videos for kids?**
You can find excellent resources on YouTube channels like Hillsong Kids, GoFish, and Seeds Family Worship. Many churches also offer online libraries of children's worship videos, and streaming platforms like RightNow Media feature extensive collections of kids' Christian content.
