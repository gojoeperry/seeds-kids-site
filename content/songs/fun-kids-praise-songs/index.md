# Fun Kids Praise Songs

# Fun Kids Praise Songs: A Guide to Joyful Worship

### Introduction
Fun kids praise songs are a powerful way to help children connect with God through music and movement. When young hearts join together in worship, they learn essential truths about God's love while creating lasting memories of faith. These engaging songs combine catchy melodies with biblical messages, making them perfect for Sunday school, family devotions, or anytime worship. By introducing fun kids praise songs early in a child's spiritual journey, we help build a foundation of joyful worship that can last a lifetime. The energy and enthusiasm children bring to praise music is contagious, often inspiring adults to worship with renewed childlike faith. Whether at home, church, or Christian school, these songs create opportunities for meaningful spiritual growth through active, participatory worship.

### Scripture
"Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise." - Psalm 98:4

"Let everything that has breath praise the Lord. Praise the Lord!" - Psalm 150:6

## Worship Ideas
1. "Every Move I Make" - Have children create motions showing how their whole body can praise God (jumping, clapping, spinning). This song teaches that worship involves our entire being.

2. "This Little Light of Mine" - Give each child a battery-operated candle or flashlight to wave during the song. Discuss how we can shine Jesus' light to others.

3. "Jesus Loves Me Rock" - Add simple hand motions and rock steps to this classic, making it upbeat and interactive while reinforcing God's love.

### Devotional Thought
Just as birds sing their beautiful songs each morning, God created us to praise Him with joy and enthusiasm. When we worship God through fun praise songs, we're showing Him how much we love Him, just like giving a big hug to someone special. Jesus loves to hear children's voices raised in worship!

Prayer: "Dear Jesus, thank you for giving us music and songs to praise you. Help us to worship you with happy hearts today and always. Amen."

### FAQs

### What age group is best for learning praise songs?
Children as young as two can begin learning simple praise songs with motions. Preschoolers through elementary age children especially enjoy active worship songs. Adjust the complexity of motions and lyrics based on your group's age and abilities.

### How can I teach new praise songs effectively?
Start by breaking down the song into small, manageable parts. Teach the motions first, then add words gradually. Use repetition and make it fun by turning it into a game. Remember to explain what the lyrics mean in child-friendly terms.

### Where can I find quality kids praise songs?
Many Christian publishers offer children's worship albums and videos. Popular resources include Hillsong Kids, Yancy Ministries, and Seeds Family Worship. Your local Christian bookstore or online platforms like YouTube Kids also provide numerous options for age-appropriate praise music.
