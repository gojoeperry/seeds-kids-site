### FAQs

### What age should I start teaching Bible songs to my child?
You can start introducing Bible songs from birth! Babies respond to melodies even before they understand words. Singing gentle Bible songs during feeding, bedtime, or playtime creates positive associations with faith-based music.

### How can I make Bible songs more engaging for active toddlers?
Incorporate movement and props! Use stuffed animals, percussion instruments, or scarves. Create simple choreography that matches the lyrics. Make it interactive by having children suggest movements or take turns leading.
