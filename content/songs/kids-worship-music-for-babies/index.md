1. "Jesus Loves Me" with gentle swaying - Hold your baby close and sway side to side while singing this classic tune

### FAQs

### What age should I start playing worship music for my baby?
You can start playing worship music for your baby from birth! Babies can hear music even in the womb, and gentle worship songs can be calming for newborns and help establish positive sleep associations.

### How can I make worship music part of our daily routine?
Incorporate worship songs into regular activities like diaper changes, bath time, or bedtime routines. Keep sessions short (5-10 minutes) and consistent. Use simple hand motions and facial expressions to keep your baby engaged.

### Where can I find age-appropriate worship music for babies?
Look for Christian lullaby collections, kids' worship albums with gentle arrangements, or baby-friendly versions of familiar hymns. Popular artists like Chris Tomlin and Hillsong have created special albums for little ones. Many streaming services also offer curated playlists specifically for baby worship time.
