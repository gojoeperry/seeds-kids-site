1. "Jesus' Love Is Like a Rainbow" - Have children make rainbow motions with their arms while singing about God's promises.

2. "God's Army" - March in place, salute, and turn around as kids learn about putting on the armor of God.

### FAQs
**What age group is best for action songs worship?**
Action songs are typically most effective for children ages 2-10, but can be adapted for any age. Preschoolers especially love the combination of music and movement, while older children can grasp deeper meanings in the lyrics.

**How can I incorporate action songs at home?**
Start with a daily "praise time" during morning routines or before bedtime. Play your action songs for kids CD during car rides, cleaning time, or as an energy break during homeschool. Make it a natural part of your family's rhythm.

**Where can I find quality action songs for worship?**
Look for CDs from established Christian children's music publishers like Hillsong Kids, Yancy Ministries, or Go Fish. Many churches also carry children's worship resources in their bookstores.
