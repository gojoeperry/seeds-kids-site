### FAQs

Q: What age should I start singing spiritual songs to my baby?
A: You can start from birth! Babies recognize voices and melodies even in the womb. Singing spiritual songs during pregnancy and continuing through infancy creates positive associations with worship and helps establish comforting bedtime routines.

Q: How can I make worship songs engaging for my baby?
A: Incorporate gentle movements, use soft touching motions, maintain eye contact, and smile while singing. Keep sessions short and watch for your baby's engagement cues. Make it a regular part of daily routines like diaper changes or bedtime.

Q: Where can I find appropriate worship songs for babies?
A: Look for Christian lullaby collections, children's worship CDs, or church nursery playlists. Many streaming services offer dedicated channels for baby worship songs. Your local Christian bookstore likely has baby-friendly worship resources, and many churches provide song suggestions for their nursery ministry.
