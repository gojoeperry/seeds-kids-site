## Worship Ideas
1. Create a "Bible Story Parade" where kids march and act out songs about Noah, Daniel, or David
2. Play "Freeze Dance" with bible story songs, having children strike poses that match the story when music stops
3. Make simple instruments from household items (rice shakers, box drums) to play along with radio songs about Jesus

### Devotional Thought
Just as David played his harp and sang songs to God, we can use music to show our love for Jesus. When we sing bible stories, we're telling God how much we care about His Word and helping others learn about His amazing love. God loves to hear children's voices lifted in praise!

### FAQs
**What age group is bible story songs radio best for?**
Bible story songs radio is typically ideal for children ages 2-10, with most programs specifically targeting preschool and early elementary ages. The songs use age-appropriate language and concepts that young children can understand and enjoy.
