## Worship Ideas
1. "Jesus Loves Me" with sign language - Teach simple hand motions for key words like "love," "Jesus," and "Bible"
2. "This Little Light of Mine" with finger lights - Give each child a small LED finger light to wave during the chorus
3. "Father Abraham" with full-body movements - Get kids moving with this classic action song that builds energy and excitement while teaching about faith

### FAQs

**What age should children start learning worship songs?**
Children can start learning simple worship songs as early as age 2-3. Even babies benefit from hearing christian music! Start with short, repetitive songs and gradually introduce more complex ones as they grow.

**How can I make worship time more engaging for kids?**
Add movement, instruments, or visual aids to keep children engaged. Use hand motions, dance moves, or simple percussion instruments. Create picture cards showing key words or concepts from the songs. Make it interactive and fun!

**Where can I find quality Christian songs for kids?**
Look for resources from trusted Christian publishers like Lifeway Kids, Hillsong Kids, or Seeds Family Worship. Many offer free content on YouTube or streaming platforms. Your local Christian bookstore likely has CDs and DVDs designed specifically for children's worship.
