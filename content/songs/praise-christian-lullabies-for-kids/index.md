## Worship Ideas
1. "Jesus Loves Me" Bedtime Version: Sing this classic hymn slowly and softly, adding gentle swaying motions
2. "God's Love is Like a Blanket": Create motions of wrapping yourself in a blanket while singing about God's protective love
3. "Goodnight, My God": A simple call-and-response song where parents sing "Goodnight" and children respond "My God," adding personal thank-you prayers between verses

### FAQs

### What age should I start using Christian lullabies with my child?
You can begin incorporating Christian lullabies from birth! Babies respond to music from their earliest days, and establishing this spiritual routine early helps create positive sleep associations and faith foundations.

### How can I make bedtime worship more engaging for my active child?
Try incorporating gentle movements like rocking or swaying, use soft stuffed animals as worship props, or create a special "worship corner" with soft pillows and battery-operated candles. Keep activities calm but interactive to help transition to sleep.

### Where can I find quality Christian lullaby resources?
Look for Christian children's music on platforms like Spotify or YouTube Kids, check Christian bookstores for bedtime worship CDs, or ask your church's children's ministry for recommendations. Many Christian artists create specific bedtime albums perfect for little ones.
