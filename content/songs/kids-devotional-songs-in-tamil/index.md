1. "Yesu En Nanban" (Jesus My Friend) - Include hand motions showing friendship and love while singing about Jesus being our best friend.

2. "Devan Nallar" (God is Good) - Create a simple circle dance where children can clap and move while praising God's goodness.

### FAQs

### What age is appropriate for learning Tamil devotional songs?
Children as young as 2-3 years can start learning simple Tamil devotional songs. The repetitive nature of children's songs makes them perfect for toddlers and preschoolers, while older children can learn more complex songs with deeper spiritual meanings.

### How can I teach Tamil devotional songs to children who don't speak Tamil fluently?
Start with simple, repetitive choruses and explain the meanings in their primary language. Use pictures or actions to illustrate the songs' messages, and practice pronunciation slowly. Consider providing written lyrics with both Tamil and English (or other language) translations.
