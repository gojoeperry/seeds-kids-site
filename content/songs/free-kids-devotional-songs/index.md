## Worship Ideas
1. "Jesus Loves Me" with Sign Language
Teach simple hand motions to represent love, Jesus, and strength while singing this classic.

2. "This Little Light of Mine" Action Song
Have children hold up their "light" (pointer finger) and act out hiding under a bushel, letting it shine, and not letting Satan blow it out.

3. "Father Abraham" Movement Song
Get kids moving with this energetic song that includes marching, arm movements, and full-body participation.

### FAQs

### What age group are these devotional songs best suited for?
Most free kids devotional songs are designed for children ages 2-10, but can be adapted for both younger and older kids. Toddlers might focus more on the motions, while older children can better understand the lyrics and biblical meanings.

### How can I incorporate devotional songs into our daily routine?
Try starting the day with a worship song during breakfast, using them during car rides, or making them part of bedtime routine. Consistency helps children learn the songs and creates positive spiritual habits.
