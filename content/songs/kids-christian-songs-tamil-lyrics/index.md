## Worship Ideas
1. "Yesu En Nesar" (Jesus My Friend) - Have children form a circle and take turns sharing what makes Jesus their friend while singing the chorus.

2. "Devan Nallavar" (God is Good) - Create simple hand motions to match the lyrics, like pointing up for "Devan" and making a thumbs up for "Nallavar."

3. "Parisuthar" (Holy One) - Use percussion instruments like tambourines or bells while singing, encouraging children to keep rhythm.

### FAQs

### What age should children start learning Tamil Christian songs?
Children can start learning Tamil Christian songs as early as age 2-3. Simple choruses with repetitive phrases are perfect for toddlers, while older children can handle more complex lyrics and meanings.

### How can I teach Tamil Christian songs to kids who don't speak Tamil fluently?
Start with simple, short phrases and provide English translations. Use visual aids, hand motions, and plenty of repetition. Break down the lyrics into smaller parts and practice regularly. Consider creating flashcards with Tamil lyrics and their meanings.
