# 50 Action Songs For Kids

# 50 Action Songs for Kids: Making Worship Fun and Meaningful

### Introduction
Teaching children to worship through 50 action songs for kids creates an engaging and memorable way for young hearts to connect with God. These dynamic worship experiences combine physical movement with spiritual truth, helping children express their faith naturally and joyfully. When kids participate in action songs, they're not just moving their bodies – they're actively engaging their hearts and minds in praise. The combination of music, movement, and biblical messages makes these 50 action songs for kids particularly effective for Sunday School, children's church, or family devotion time. By incorporating movement into worship, children develop a deeper understanding of God's love while building lasting spiritual foundations through fun and interactive praise.

### Scripture
"Let everything that has breath praise the Lord!" (Psalm 150:6)
"Make a joyful noise unto the Lord, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise." (Psalm 98:4)

## Worship Ideas
1. "Jesus' Love is Bubbling Over" - Children crouch down low and slowly rise while wiggling fingers, then jump up with arms raised when "bubbling over."

2. "This Little Light of Mine" - Hold up index finger as a "light," wave it high and low, and hide it under the other hand for "don't let Satan blow it out."

3. "Father Abraham" - Start with basic movements (right arm, left arm) and gradually add more actions, teaching kids about having many sons and being one of them.

### Devotional Thought
Just as our bodies can move and dance to show happiness, our hearts can jump with joy when we think about God's love. Jesus wants us to worship Him with our whole selves – our voices, our movements, and our hearts. When we sing and move in worship, we're showing God how much we love Him, just like King David danced before the Lord.

Dear God, thank you for giving us songs to sing and ways to move as we worship You. Help us remember that every part of us can praise You. Amen.

### FAQs

Q: What age group are these action songs best suited for?
A: These action songs are primarily designed for children ages 3-10, though they can be adapted for younger or older kids. Preschoolers particularly enjoy the movement, while older children can grasp deeper meanings in the lyrics.

Q: How can I teach action songs to wiggly children?
A: Break down the movements into simple steps, demonstrate them clearly, and practice one portion at a time. Make it fun and remember that perfection isn't the goal – participation and joy in worship are what matter most.

Q: Where can I find resources for kids' action songs?
A: You can find action songs in children's ministry curriculum, Christian bookstores, online platforms like YouTube Kids, and through church resource websites. Many publishers offer both audio recordings and motion instruction videos.
