### FAQs

**Q: What age should I start playing Christian music for my baby?**
A: You can start playing Christian music for your baby even before birth! Babies can hear music in the womb, and continuing after birth helps create familiarity and comfort. The earlier you begin, the more natural worship music will become part of their daily life.

**Q: How can I make worship time more engaging for my baby?**
A: Incorporate gentle movements, use soft toys as props, and maintain eye contact while singing. Keep sessions short (5-10 minutes), and choose consistent times like before naps or during playtime to establish a routine.

**Q: Where can I find appropriate Christian music for babies?**
A: Look for specialized Christian lullaby albums on platforms like Spotify or Apple Music. Many Christian artists create baby-specific worship music. You can also find dedicated YouTube channels featuring gentle Christian songs for infants, or purchase CDs from Christian bookstores specifically designed for babies.
